Data & Privacy

Your clients' data, handled correctly.

We know your compliance team will ask. Here are the facts.

🔒

Full content is never stored by us

The complete message or image is analyzed and returned as a verdict. It is never written to our servers. Only a short reference snippet of up to 80 characters is retained in your advisor dashboard for audit purposes, linked to the client's internal account ID — not their name or email.

👤

No identity sent to the AI

The analysis request sent to our AI provider contains only the message content. No client name, email, advisor ID, or account details are ever included in the analysis payload. Identity and content are never linked at the point of analysis.

🧠

Not used for AI training

We use Anthropic's API to power verdicts. Under Anthropic's standard paid API policy, inputs and outputs are not used to train AI models. Your clients' submissions do not contribute to any model training dataset.

📋

Enterprise zero-retention available

If your compliance team requires a contractual zero-retention guarantee that eliminates even Anthropic's standard 30-day abuse monitoring window, we support an upgrade to Anthropic's enterprise ZDR tier on request.

For your compliance team: Full message content is never stored by DoubleCheck. A snippet of up to 80 characters is retained for your advisor audit trail.
